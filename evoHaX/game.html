Challenge 2: Agnosia

scenario

Eli's table is ready. He is confident because he has memorized basic table settings and what each item does.

Unfortunately for him, the bus boy was new and didn't set the table up properly.

limitations

As someone with agnosia, Eli does not know what an item is when it's out of context. Also, if an item is placed in the wrong context Eli will identify the item incorrectly.

challenge

Pick all of the following that Eli is most likely to do?

You are correct!

Did you know?

Eli wouldn't ask for a spoon because as far as he is concerned the utensil on the right is the spoon. He will only find out that it is a fork when he tries to eat his soup with it.

Eli won't recognize the water glass misplaced to the top left of the plate. But if it is moved to the top right he will instantly recognize it as his water glass.

Challenge 3: Prosopagnosia

scenario

Lucky guy: Eli knows several celebrities!

One of his famous friends sits down at Eli's table to chat.

Eli tries to figure out which of his celeb friends is sitting across from him so that he can greet his friend properly.

limitations

As someone with prosopagnosia he doesn't recognize faces. He relies on categorization to guess who the person is. But due to the limitations of his prospagnosia he can only process certain attributes.

challenge

Pick a celebrity, then choose attributes about that celebrity from the following categories to see if Eli can figure out which one of these famous people are sitting across from him.

Did you know?

Eli cannot differentiate skin color or eye color?

Also, the friend's profession won't help Eli because it is out of context. (Although, Eli may ask some questions about work to get more clues as to who his friend is.)

What can Eli's friends do to help?

When greeting Eli, his friends can mention their own name.
