Eli is a college student with alexia and two associated conditions: agnosia and prosopagnosia.

Alexia

with prosopagnosia

limitations

As someone with prosopagnosia he doesn't recognize faces. He relies on categorization to guess who the person is. But due to the limitations of his prospagnosia he can only process certain attributes.

scenario

Eli is sitting in a coffee shop. Lucky guy: He knows several celebrities!

One of these very famous people sits down at Eli's table to chat.

Eli tries to figure out which of his celeb friends is sitting across from him so that he can greet his friend.

challenge

Pick a celebrity, then choose attributes about that celebrity from the following categories to see if Eli can figure out which one of these famous people are sitting across from him.

Did you know?

Eli cannot differentiate skin color or eye color?

Also, the friend's profession won't help Eli because it is out of context. (Although, Eli may ask some questions about work to get more clues as to who his friend is.)

What can Eli's friends do to help?

When greeting Eli, his friends can mention their own name.
