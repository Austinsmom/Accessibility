Beyond Celiac

Join us to learn more about Celiac Disease.

December 15th
9:00am
42nd Floor Amphitheatre, Comcast Center

Speaker

Alice Bast, CEO, Beyond Celiac

Discussion Highlights

  • Be aware that more and more people are eating gluten-free, but for some, it's more than a lifestyle choice, it's a medical necessity.
  • Learn about the numerous symptoms that can be attributed to gluten that can affect workplace productivity.
  • Discover workplace accommodations and get some tangible takeaways that could help employees with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity and other gluten-related disorders.
