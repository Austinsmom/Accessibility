Tab Menu—Two Versions

View the library of solutions.

Same-page tab menus can either:

  • Show a single content panel at a time.
  • Show all content and scroll to the content requested.

After navigating to new in-page content press the tab key and notice where focus goes.

What's important is that engaging in-page navigation moves focus to the content requested via js. By assigning tabindex="-1" to the content container it can accept focus.

Use the buttons below to see each version. The js does not change.
