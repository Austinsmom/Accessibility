Our Gang

History

More famously known as "The Little Rascals", "Our Gang" was a series of comedy shorts made between 1922 and 1944 which followed the adventures of a group of poor neighborhood kids. It was unusual for both highlighting the natural behavior of kids and portraying different races as equal.
